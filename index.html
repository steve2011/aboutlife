About life after death:

Hebrews 9:27 And as it is appointed unto men once to die, but after this the judgment

Nothing we "own" in this life is ours forever. We begin and end life with no material possessions

The Bible verse "For we brought nothing into this world, and it is certain we can carry nothing out" appears in the King James Version of 1 Timothy 6:7. The verse continues, "And having food and raiment let us be therewith content. But they that will be rich fall into temptation and a snare, and into many foolish and hurtful lusts, which drown men in destruction and perdition".
